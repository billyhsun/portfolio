About Me


Hello! I'm Bill (Yuan Hong) Sun, an Engineering Science graduate from the University of Toronto, majoring in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. I am enthusiastic about using software, big data, machine learning, and the engineering approach to solve challenging problems across different disciplines, and helping to improve people's lives through technology.

I am currently working as a Machine Learning Engineer, where I develop machine learning modules and pipelines for retail and shipment forecasting solutions. Previously, from 2019 to 2022, I worked at Nuralogix as a Data Science Software Developer, working closely with the Anura personal health monitoring app and the DeepAffex (DFX) cloud service.

I have numerous software projects, and I am also doing research at University of Toronto, as showcased by my publications below. In my spare time, I am involved in numerous extracurricular activities. I actively attend technical meetups and participate in hackathons. I also like to workout, play sports, travel, photography, volunteer, and mentor younger peers.

You can reach me by email or LinkedIn. You can also read about my engineering insights and reflections in my blog.
